A framework to help schools and colleges identify the strengths and areas for improvement in their remote education provision

Applies to England

The remote education review framework has been produced to support schools and colleges in England to identify the strengths and areas for improvement in their remote education provision. It is not statutory, and you can adapt it to fit your school or college context.

The framework is for internal use, and should support detailed discussions on appropriate next steps. It can help you to meet basic requirements using the resources and tools you currently have (digital or physical) and to take your remote education provision further - with signposts to useful resources.
