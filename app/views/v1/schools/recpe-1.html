Home environment

The school is aware of the learning environment in the home and works with parents and families to understand and ensure that pupils will be able to access education at home.

The school supports pupils on how to self-regulate during remote education, including:

  • understanding their strengths and weaknesses to improve their learning
  • how to learn from home
  • how to manage their time during periods of isolation
