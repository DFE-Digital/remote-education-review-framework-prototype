Remote education review framework

This framework has been produced to support schools in England to identify the strengths and areas for improvement in their remote education offer, and to signpost them to resources that can help them improve their practice.

Who is this framework for?

This guidance is for MAT leaders, school leaders and governors in England.

Aims of the framework

This framework aims to help MAT leaders, school leaders and governors in England to:

  • identify the strengths and areas for improvement in their school or trust's remote education provision
  • find resources (including training), guidance and networks to help them improve their provision
